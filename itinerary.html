Travel Itinerary: Seoul & Japan

March 20 - March 22

  • Flight from SFO to Seoul
  • Arrival in Seoul (March 22)

March 22 - March 25 (Seoul, South Korea)

March 22

  • Check into hotel and rest.
  • Evening: Walk around Insadong for local shops and casual dining.

March 23

  • Morning: Gyeongbokgung Palace (watch the changing of the guard).
  • Afternoon: Bukchon Hanok Village for traditional Korean houses.
  • Evening: Myeongdong street food and shopping.

March 24

  • DMZ Day Trip (booked tour) – historical insights, observation points.

March 25

  • Morning: N Seoul Tower for panoramic city views.
  • Late morning/early afternoon: Flight from Seoul to Tokyo.

March 25 - April 3 (Japan: Tokyo → Osaka → Kyoto → Mt. Fuji → Tokyo)

Tokyo (March 25 - March 27)

March 25

  • Check into Tokyo hotel, relax after arrival.

March 26

  • Morning: Shibuya Crossing and Harajuku.
  • Afternoon: Meiji Shrine.

March 27

  • Morning: Asakusa and Senso-ji Temple.
  • Afternoon: Tokyo Skytree for views.

Osaka (March 28 - March 29)

March 28

  • Shinkansen from Tokyo to Osaka (2.5 hours).
  • Afternoon: Osaka Castle.
  • Evening: Dotonbori street food area (try takoyaki, okonomiyaki).

March 29

  • Day trip to Kyoto from Osaka (30 minutes by train).
  • Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), and Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.
  • Return to Osaka in the evening.

Lake Kawaguchiko / Mt. Fuji Area (March 30 - March 31)

March 30

  • Travel to Lake Kawaguchiko (train to Mishima, then rent a car for flexibility).
  • Explore Lake Kawaguchiko with views of Mt. Fuji, visit Chureito Pagoda for iconic photos.

March 31

  • Return car and head back to Tokyo in the evening.

Tokyo (April 1 - April 3)

April 1

  • Free day to explore or visit any missed sites in Tokyo.

April 2

  • Relax, final shopping, and souvenirs in Tokyo.

April 3

  • Evening Flight from Tokyo to SFO.
