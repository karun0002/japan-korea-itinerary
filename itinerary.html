Travel Itinerary: Seoul & Japan

March 20 - March 22

  • Flight from SFO to Seoul
  • Arrival in Seoul (March 22)

March 22 - March 25 (Seoul, South Korea)

March 22

  • Check into hotel and rest.
  • Evening: Walk around Insadong for local shops and casual dining.

March 23

  • Morning: Gyeongbokgung Palace (watch the changing of the guard ceremony).
  • Afternoon: Bukchon Hanok Village for traditional Korean houses.
  • Evening: Myeongdong street food and shopping.

March 24

  • DMZ Day Trip – historical insights, observation points.

March 25

  • Morning: N Seoul Tower for panoramic views.
  • Afternoon: Explore Yeouido Park or Seokchon Lake for cherry blossom viewing.
  • Evening: Wrap up any last experiences or shopping.

March 26 - April 3 (Japan: Osaka → Kyoto → Lake Kawaguchiko/Mt. Fuji → Tokyo)

Osaka (March 26 - March 27)

  • Fly from Seoul to Osaka, Japan.
  • Check into hotel in Osaka.
  • Evening: Explore Dotonbori for street food and night views.

March 27

  • Morning: Osaka Castle and nearby Osaka Castle Park for cherry blossoms.
  • Afternoon: Visit Kuromon Ichiba Market for food exploration.
  • Evening: Namba area and Shinsaibashi shopping street.

Kyoto (March 28 - March 29)

  • Morning: Fushimi Inari Shrine (thousands of red torii gates).
  • Afternoon: Kiyomizu-dera Temple and Maruyama Park (excellent for cherry blossoms).
  • Evening: Stroll through Gion District for traditional teahouses.

March 29

  • Morning: Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion).
  • Afternoon: Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and Tenryu-ji Temple (cherry blossoms and scenic gardens).
  • Evening: Head back to Osaka or stay in Kyoto.

Lake Kawaguchiko / Mt. Fuji Area (March 30)

  • Travel to Lake Kawaguchiko (train to Mishima, then rent a car).
  • Cherry blossom viewing around Lake Kawaguchiko with Mt. Fuji as the backdrop.
  • Visit Chureito Pagoda for classic Mt. Fuji and cherry blossom views.
  • Evening: Return to Tokyo.

Tokyo (March 31 - April 3)

March 31

  • Morning: Explore Shibuya Crossing and Harajuku.
  • Afternoon: Yoyogi Park or Ueno Park (popular Tokyo cherry blossom spots).
  • Evening: Dinner and nightlife in Shinjuku.

April 1

  • Morning: Tsukiji Outer Market for breakfast and food tasting.
  • Afternoon: Asakusa and Senso-ji Temple.
  • Evening: Sumida River Park for cherry blossom viewing and Tokyo Skytree views.

April 2

  • Free day to explore or visit any missed spots in Tokyo.
  • Suggested areas: Chidorigafuchi for cherry blossoms or Akihabara for shopping.

April 3

  • Final shopping or relax in Tokyo.
  • Evening: Flight from Tokyo to SFO.
