Must-Do List by City

Seoul, South Korea

  • Gyeongbokgung Palace: Watch the changing of the guard and explore the historic palace grounds.
  • Bukchon Hanok Village: Walk through traditional Korean houses with scenic views.
  • Myeongdong: Try local street food and shop.
  • DMZ Day Trip: Insightful tour of the Korean Demilitarized Zone.
  • N Seoul Tower: Panoramic views of Seoul.
  • Yeouido Park or Seokchon Lake: Cherry blossom viewing (seasonal).

Osaka, Japan

  • Osaka Castle and Osaka Castle Park: Beautiful cherry blossom viewing in spring, historical castle with gardens.
  • Dotonbori: Famous for street food, neon lights, and bustling atmosphere.
  • Kuromon Ichiba Market: Local food market with fresh seafood, snacks, and unique Osaka eats.
  • Namba and Shinsaibashi: Shopping streets and vibrant nightlife.

Kyoto, Japan

  • Fushimi Inari Shrine: Iconic shrine with thousands of red torii gates.
  • Kiyomizu-dera Temple: Historic temple with views of cherry blossoms in spring.
  • Maruyama Park: Popular cherry blossom viewing spot in Kyoto.
  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion): Scenic golden temple surrounded by beautiful gardens.
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove: Walk through towering bamboo forests.
  • Gion District: Traditional area known for teahouses and geisha culture.

Lake Kawaguchiko / Mt. Fuji Area, Japan

  • Lake Kawaguchiko: Cherry blossoms with Mt. Fuji in the background.
  • Chureito Pagoda: Iconic view of Mt. Fuji framed by cherry blossoms.

Tokyo, Japan

  • Shibuya Crossing: Experience one of the busiest pedestrian crossings in the world.
  • Harajuku: Unique fashion, quirky shops, and pop culture vibe.
  • Yoyogi Park or Ueno Park: Prime cherry blossom viewing locations.
  • Tsukiji Outer Market: Fresh seafood and breakfast options.
  • Asakusa and Senso-ji Temple: Historic temple and bustling market street.
  • Tokyo Skytree: Observation tower with panoramic city views.
  • Sumida River Park: Cherry blossoms with a view of the Tokyo Skytree.
  • Chidorigafuchi: Picturesque cherry blossom spot near the Imperial Palace.
  • Akihabara: Electronics, anime, and gaming district.
